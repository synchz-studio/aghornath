About

Aghornath (Girnar Aghor Akhada)
ॐ Aghornath .

Fearless Awareness — Aghor

Aghornath is a living akhada rooted in the ancient Aghor tradition of Mount Girnar.
Here, seekers walk the path of awareness beyond fear and aversion.

Under the guidance of Guru Ajit Nath Aghori, the akhada preserves authentic tantric and yogic practices while remaining grounded in direct experience and service to humanity.
Our purpose: dissolve inner darkness, serve beings as forms of Shiva, and live with the clarity that everything is sacred.

Girnar has long been home to ascetics from both the Aghor and Nath Sampradāya.
Aghornath continues that living current today.


Path of the Skull

To face death is to conquer illusion. The skull is not a symbol of fear but of awakening beyond form.


Living Aghor

Beyond purity and impurity, the Aghori walks in oneness — seeing the same divine light in all.

Lineage - (Paramparā)

Lineage

Nath–Aghor Paramparā
नाथ–अघोर परम्परा


Guru Ajit Nath Aghori belongs to the Nath Sampradāya (tracing to Matsyendranath and Gorakhnath), associated with the Satnath Panth.

His Guru is Sevanath Ji Maharaj.
This living paramparā is transmission-based: the Guru awakens understanding through presence; the disciple sustains it through discipline.
